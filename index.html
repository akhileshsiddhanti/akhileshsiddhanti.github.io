About

I am currently pursuing Masters of Science in Computer Science at Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta. I completed B.E.(Hons.) Computer Science and M.Sc. (Hons.) Mathematics from Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS) Pilani K. K. Birla Goa Campus in May 2019.

During my undergraduate days, I have worked in the area of Cryptography, specifically in the area of stream ciphers. During the summers, I interned at Indian Statistical Institute (ISI), Kolkata and Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore on various fault and side-channel attacks and Time-Memory-Data Tradeoff Attacks on lightweight stream and block ciphers. This has resulted in several publications in leading conferences and journals.
